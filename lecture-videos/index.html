Lecture Videos

The following links point to video files in mp4 format. Please do not stream them; view them locally by saving them to your computer. To do so, simply context-click (which is the middle finger click if you click primarily with your index finger), then choose to save the video. Your browser may provide you with more than one option for saving. Try each until you’re able to save the mp4 video to your machine.
