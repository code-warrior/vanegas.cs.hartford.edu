Grading Conversion Table

This is the grading conversion table for courses I teach in the Dept of Computing Sciences at The University of Hartford. If your grade sits between two other grades, then your participation–including attendance–dictates whether your grade becomes the ceiling or the floor. For example, if your grade is 91.5%, meaning it’s between A and A−, and your participation and attendance in the course is satisfactory, then your grade would be A.
