Roy Vanegas’s Overtally Policy

CS-114

Overtallies into cs 114 occur on a case-by-case basis during the beginning of the semester, and are limited to two or three for the semester. If you’re not a cs/cse/hcid major, or are not an engineering major at ceta, then no overtally is possible.

If, however, you do fall into either of the previously-mentioned categories, reach out to me via email 4 business days before classes start with the following information:

  • Number of credits you’ve earned thus far
  • Class standing (First year, Sophomore, Junior, or Senior)
  • Major
  • School in which you’re enrolled
  • Student ID

Regardless of whether an overtally is granted, attend class as though you’ve been registered, and speak to me on the first day the class meets. I also suggest you keep shopping for classes, in the event that you’re not overtallied into the course.
